### The Hacker Dictionary

*   **Clean Coins**: Crypto with no history of illegal use.
*   **Cold Storage**: Storing crypto keys offline (Paper/Hardware).
*   **DDOS**: Distributed Denial of Service. Flooding a site to crash it.
*   **De-anonymization**: The process of revealing a user's true identity.
*   **Dox**: Publishing someone's private info (Name, Address) online.
*   **Drop**: A physical location to receive goods.
*   **Escrow**: A third party holding funds until a deal is complete.
*   **Exit Node**: The last Tor node where traffic enters the clear web.
*   **Exit Scam**: When a market or vendor suddenly disappears with all user funds.
*   **FUD**: Fear, Uncertainty, and Doubt. Spreading fake bad news.
*   **Honeypot**: A trap setup by law enforcement (e.g., a fake market).
*   **Mixer / Tumbler**: A service that mixes coins to obscure their origin.
*   **OpSec**: Operational Security. The process of protecting information.
*   **PGP**: Pretty Good Privacy. Encryption standard.
*   **Phishing**: A fake site designed to steal credentials.
*   **Tainted Coins**: Crypto linked to crime, blacklisted by exchanges.
*   **VPN**: Virtual Private Network. (Controversial with Tor).
