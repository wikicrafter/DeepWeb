### Real-Time Chat

**Encryption is NOT Automatic:**
Most IRC (Internet Relay Chat) servers are unencrypted text. The admin sees everything.
*   **Solution**: OTR (Off-the-Record) Messaging.
*   **What it does**: End-to-end encryption for instant messaging. It provides *authentication* (you know who you are talking to) and *deniability* (messages cannot be digitally signed to prove you wrote them later).

**Chat Services:**
*   **Cryptodog**: Web-based encrypted chat.
*   **XMPP (Jabber)**: The standard for private messaging. Use clients like **Pidgin** or **CoyIM** with OTR enabled.
*   **Matrix**: A modern, decentralized alternative to XMPP.
