### Search Indexers

**The Challenge:**
Standard crawlers follow hyperlinks. Dark web sites often don't link to each other, and many are behind login walls.

**Active Engines:**
*   **Ahmia.fi**: The only search engine that indexes .onion sites accessible from the clear web (for viewing). It filters out abuse material.
*   **Torch**: Massive database, but lots of dead links and ads.
*   **DuckDuckGo (Onion)**: Great for privacy, but primarily searches the *Clear Web* from Tor. It does NOT index onion sites deeply.
